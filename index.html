Biography

I am a PhD candidate at School of Computer Science and Engineering, the University of New South Wales, supervised by Prof. Lina Yao and Prof. Salil Kanhere. Before that, I received B.S. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Jilin University, in 2018.
You may see my latest CV here ( 2021-05 edition).

Research Interest

My research interests mainly focus on machine learning and data mining, especially on the application of generative networks in different real world scenarios. My current topics are summarized as follows:

  • Consumer Behavior Analysis
  • Human Activity Recognition
  • Zero-shot Learning
